Noteworthy Alumni

The department administers the graduate field of plant pathology and plant-microbe biology with numerous noteworthy alumni. Among them are:

  • Private practitioners Cynthia Westcott and P.P. Pirone
  • Science policy advocate and scholar J. G. Horsfall
  • World Food Prize winner John Niederhauser
  • Eminent mycologist R.P. Korf
  • World-renowned nematologist W.F. Mai.

You can access a more comprehensive documentation on the history of the Geneva department by J. E. Hunter and of the Ithaca department by G. C. Kent and A. G. Newhall.
